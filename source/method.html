Research
at the Center
Cutting edge science

The Longevity Center has experience in clinical trial design, and data processing. Our on-site Principle Investigator and research team are part of a large network of state of the art academic and industrial clinical research facilities. Our science helps to further advance the study of preventive medicine and longevity.

Our internal facility can asses most know biomarkers of aging. These are promising experimental tools to estimate lifespan and longevity. Our experts can interpret and integrate these data in our client’s health assessments, as an ongoing research program, that ultimately will benefit them.

As our research develops and more client’s decide to participate, the center’s data base will grow, allowing our scientists and selected world wide renowned experts to build the future of the longevity science.
